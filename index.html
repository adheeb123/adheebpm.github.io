About Us

TOIVO STAR is a reliable contractor specialized in offshore and onshore oil and gas industry and Facility services. Headquartered in Abu Dhabi-UAE, TOIVO STAR's core values-ethics, commitment, quality, people, culture, integrity, innovation and substainability.

HOW WE WORK

TOIVO STAR’s success lies in its commitment to running events with professionalism and expertise, and in identifying strongly with their outcome. This sense of responsibility means we always strive to add value and are pro-active in solving problems and in adopting client procedures.

We specially select teams for every project, to ensure each event gets the attention of the people who have the most relevant technical skills, languages and knowledge. TOIVO STAR also draws on its extensive Local network to access the best know-how, contacts and partnerships from around the world.

The volume of business we generate gives us – and by implication our clients – greater bargaining power with suppliers, and in several cases we have long-term contracts in place at advantageous rates.
